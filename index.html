My Home

Wild Stephs live in Queens. Queens is an ideal borough for wild Stephs because it is more quiet than Manhattan and Brooklyn. Stephs love to nest, and spend lots of time in their homes. Stephs also love to eat, and 321 restaurants deliver to their Astoria habitat.

My Diet

Like many New Yorkers, Stephs primarily eat pizza. Despite the vast amount of food options available, 70% of a wild Stephs food intake is pizza. Stephs are animals that dislike disappointment so much they only alot 30% of their diet to diversity.

Pizza is great

My Loves

A strange evolutional trait allow Stephs to see the world in degrees of fluffiness. While literal fluff is helpful to getting into a Stephs habitat, one can be fluffy without fur. This is what Stephs refer to as "fluffy on the inside". It is a very rare and sought after trait for which Stephs have a keen eye.

Fluff is life
